Home

Welkom to my home page. As you can read above, my name is Floris De Smedt. I got my bachlor degree of electonics and ICT in 2007 at the KHK, and in june 2009 I graduated as a master in Industial science Electronics and ICT. Since october 2009 I'm working for the EAVISE research group.

Currently I'm a PhD assistent, working on a PhD about the detection of abnormal behaviour on public transportation. This project combines two knowledge domains, namely computer vision (focussing on object detection/tracking and action recognition) and knowledge representation. On the other pages of this site, you will find more information about my research topics.

Next to my work, I'm also engaged with scouting, indoor soccer and card magic. I was an active member in my local scouting group at Lubbeek for 19 years whereof 8 years as a leader. This year I moved more to the background. Card magic is a hobby I began with almost 2 years ago. By following many sites on the internet, watching a huge amount of youtube movies and reading a lot of books I'm beginning to understand the art of astonishment with cards. One of the things I learned is that the way one performs a trick is far more important as been able to execute the sleights individualy. Just by creating a simple story around the trick that distracts the spectators will let you look like a master performing a trick doable by a 7 year old.

So, this was my introduction of who I am. Feel free to contact me.
